Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when interacting with this service.

Information Collected

This service may collect phone numbers and message content solely for the purpose of delivering SMS or MMS communications. No sensitive personal information is intentionally collected.

Use of Information

Information collected is used only to operate and maintain messaging functionality, including message delivery, troubleshooting, and compliance with carrier or platform requirements.

Third-Party Services

This service uses third-party providers, including Twilio, to send and receive messages. These providers may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Message data may be retained for operational or compliance purposes and is not sold, shared, or distributed for marketing or advertising purposes.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. Additional assistance may be requested by replying HELP.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without prior notice.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the service operator directly.
